Description:

The Nissan Laurel (C130) is a mid-size luxury car produced by Nissan in Japan from 1972 to 1977. It was the third generation of the Laurel series. The C130 Laurel featured a sleek and elegant design, offering a comfortable and luxurious driving experience. With its range of engine options and advanced features for its time, the Laurel C130 was well-received in the market, solidifying its reputation as a premium vehicle in Nissan's lineup.
